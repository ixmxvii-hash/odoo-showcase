Introduction: A Growing Restaurant Chain at a Crossroads

This fast-growing restaurant chain operates multiple locations across Lagos, Nigeria. Known for delivering quality food and exceptional customer service, the company built a strong brand presence throughout the region. As demand increased and new locations opened, their operations quickly outgrew the patchwork of spreadsheets and disconnected systems they relied on.

With ambitious plans to expand across West Africa, the leadership team knew they needed a unified system that could scale with their growth and provide real-time visibility across all locations.

The Challenge: Disconnected Systems Holding Back Growth

The restaurant chain's operations were managed through a fragmented mix of Excel spreadsheets, standalone POS systems, and separate accounting software. Each restaurant location ran different applications that didn't communicate with one another, creating major operational blind spots:

Inventory chaos Tracking inventory across multiple locations via spreadsheets led to stock discrepancies and significant losses
No financial visibility The POS system didn't integrate with accounting, making financial reporting slow and error-prone
Vendor management gaps No centralized way to track vendor deliveries or manage payment schedules
Operational bottlenecks Leadership lacked real-time data to make informed decisions about purchasing, staffing, or expansion

These inefficiencies weren't just frustrating–they were costing money and preventing the company from scaling confidently into new markets.

The Solution: A Phased Odoo Implementation

Rather than disrupting operations with a complete system overhaul, ICIT Solutions recommended a phased implementation approach that would allow the restaurant chain to transition gradually while maintaining business continuity.

Phase 1: Financial Foundation

  • Migrated from Excel to Odoo for centralized inventory management
  • Implemented Odoo Accounting across all restaurant locations
  • Established a single source of truth for financials and stock levels

Phase 2: Integration & Production

  • Integrated existing POS system with Odoo to sync daily transactions automatically
  • Deployed Odoo Manufacturing (MRP) module to manage food production and recipes
  • Enabled real-time reporting across inventory, sales, and accounting

Phase 3: Complete Platform Unification

  • Retired legacy POS system and migrated to Odoo Point of Sale
  • Launched Odoo eCommerce for online ordering
  • Achieved full operational integration across all locations
Technical Infrastructure: As a Microsoft partner, ICIT Solutions provided additional value by setting up a dedicated Azure cloud environment to host Odoo, giving the client full autonomy and control over their infrastructure.

Results: Operational Excellence and Accelerated Growth

Since going live with Odoo, this restaurant chain has transformed from a regionally fragmented operation into a unified, data-driven business positioned for expansion.

  • 30% revenue increase driven by improved inventory efficiency and reduced waste
  • Centralized management of inventory, sales, and accounting across all locations
  • Online ordering capability launched without adding headcount
  • Real-time visibility into operations enabling faster, smarter decisions
  • Vendor accountability with full tracking of deliveries and payment schedules
  • Expansion-ready platform supporting growth across West Africa

The company now operates with significantly less overhead while serving more customers across more channels. The team can do more with less, and leadership has the confidence and data they need to open new locations throughout the region.
